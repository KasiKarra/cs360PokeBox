About

This is a Candybox-style Pokemon web game. You choose your starter pokemon and begin the journey to catch them all. Different stages will allow you to battle pokemon and earn points to be able to evolve them and buy stronger Pokeballs to fit in your ever growing backpack. Points will be generated over time, and having different Pokemon can influence this rate. We hope you enjoy this game!

Pikachu

It evolves from Pichu when leveled up with high friendship and evolves into Raichu when exposed to a Thunder Stone. However, the starter Pikachu in Pokémon Yellow will refuse to evolve into Raichu unless it is traded and evolved on another save file.
